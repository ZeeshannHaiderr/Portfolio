About

Engineering intelligence
into silicon

I'm a final-year Electrical Engineering student at NUST, Islamabad — building at the bleeding edge of hardware AI and embedded systems.

My flagship project synthesizes a custom Convolutional Neural Network directly onto a Xilinx Virtex-7 FPGA via Vivado HLS, enabling real-time pneumonia diagnosis without cloud dependency — then pipes results into MedGemma 1.5B for automated clinical report generation.

Beyond AI hardware, I engineer FreeRTOS-based real-time control systems, computer vision pipelines with optical flow tracking, ML forecasting models, and full IoT solutions. I've interned at the SoC Lab at NUST on RTL design and Edge-AI research.

Seeking M.S. / Ph.D. research assistantships where I can push the boundary of edge computing and AI hardware acceleration.
